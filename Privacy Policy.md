# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: February 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how EasyAgent (the "Application"), provided by Drecom (the "Service Provider"), collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile app and related services.

By using EasyAgent, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

## 1) Information We Collect

### a) Account and business profile information

- Email address (used for sign-in and account management)
- Company name and business sector
- Subscription/tier status

### b) Calling and messaging information

- Phone numbers used for inbound/outbound calls
- Call metadata (time, duration, status, direction)
- Call recordings, transcripts, and AI call analysis
- SMS/message content and metadata sent or received through your connected numbers

### c) Appointment and CRM-style information

- Appointment details (date/time, title, notes)
- Client information you provide (name, phone, email)
- Contacts you create in the app

### d) Device and technical information

- Device model, OS version, app version, IP address
- App usage and diagnostics data
- Push notification token and device identifiers used for notifications

### e) Optional permissions and related data

- **Microphone**: Required to place/handle voice calls
- **Notifications**: Used to deliver call, message, and appointment alerts
- **Contacts (optional)**: Used to read/add/edit your device contacts when you choose those features
- **Tracking permission on iOS (ATT)**: Requested before enabling advertising/tracking features (where applicable)

EasyAgent does not collect precise GPS location for core functionality.
